Airline Satisfaction Report

Report Overview


In an industry that has seen an explosion in passenger numbers over the recent decades, customer retention is more important than ever. Customers are flying in high numbers and at Happy Flyers, we want them to fly with us once they have tried with us. Satisfaction is not always easy to control and in some situations, increasing satisfaction in a particular variable will prove cost prohibitive. For example, Leg room satisfaction in economy will inevitably be lower than leg room satisfaction in business class. However while high income flyers once ruled the sky, as you can see below, market share of high income flyers has dropped from over 90% in the 1970s to 40% in 2019. We need to look to increase overall satisfaction across our customer base.



Demographics of Our Flyers


The Data Science team here at Happy Flyers have reviewed survey results of over one hundred thousand recent Happy Flyers and analysed 14 different variables across a range of customer demographics.
